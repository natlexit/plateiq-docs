# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 4, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **PlateIQ** ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it.

By accessing or using the PlateIQ mobile application (the "App"), you agree to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this policy.

**IMPORTANT NOTICE REGARDING INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS:**
PlateIQ operates globally, and our servers are located in the **United States (Google Cloud Platform)**. By using this App, you explicitly acknowledge and consent to the transfer, storage, and processing of your personal data in the United States, a jurisdiction that may have different data protection laws than your country of residence.

## 2. Information We Collect

To provide you with our AI-powered calorie tracking and nutrition planning services, we collect the following types of information:

### A. Personal Information You Disclose to Us
* **Account Data:** Email address, nickname/name, and password (encrypted).
* **Social Login Data:** If you log in via Google or Apple, we collect your email and public profile information associated with that provider.

### B. Health and Biometric Data (Sensitive Information)
To calculate your daily calorie needs and track progress, we collect:
* Gender, Age, Height, Current Weight, and Target Weight.
* Activity level and Dietary goals (e.g., "Lose Weight", "Gain Muscle").
* Food logs (meals consumed, calories, macronutrients).
* Allergies and dietary restrictions.

### C. User Content (AI Analysis)
* **Food Photos:** When you upload photos of meals, they are transmitted to our servers to be analyzed by Artificial Intelligence algorithms for food recognition and calorie estimation.

### D. Automatically Collected Information
* **Device Information:** Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers.
* **Location Data:** We do **not** collect precise GPS coordinates. We collect your general **Timezone** (e.g., "Asia/Kuala_Lumpur") and IP address to ensure meal logs correspond to your local time.
* **Payment Information:** We use **Stripe** to process payments. We **do not** store your credit card details. We only store a token ID and the status of your subscription (e.g., "Active", "Expired").

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for the following purposes:
1.  **Service Provision:** To create your account, calculate your nutrition plan, and track your progress.
2.  **AI Improvement:** To analyze food photos and improve the accuracy of our calorie recognition algorithms.
3.  **Communication:** To send you technical notices, updates, and security alerts.
4.  **Analytics:** To understand how users interact with the App (using tools like Google Analytics/Firebase) to improve user experience.
5.  **Legal Compliance:** To comply with applicable laws and regulations.

## 4. International Data Transfer & Storage

**Location of Servers:**
All user data is stored securely on **Google Cloud Platform (GCP)** servers located in the **United States (region: us-central1)**.

**Consent to Transfer:**
If you are accessing the App from the European Union (EU), Russia, Asia, or any other region with laws governing personal data collection, use, and disclosure that differ from United States laws, please be advised that through your continued use of the App, you are transferring your personal information to the United States and you consent to that transfer.

**Note for Users in the Russian Federation:**
By registering in the App, you acknowledge that the database collecting your personal data is located in the United States. You explicitly consent to the cross-border transfer of your data to the US for the purpose of contract performance (providing the App's service).

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy.

**Delete Account:**
You have the right to delete your account and all associated data at any time. You can do this directly within the App:
* Go to **Profile** > Scroll down to the "Danger Zone" > Tap **Delete Account**.
* This action is irreversible and will immediately remove your personal data from our active databases.

## 6. Security of Your Data

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information (including HTTPS encryption for data in transit). However, please remember that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

## 7. Your Data Protection Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:
* **The right to access:** You have the right to request copies of your personal data.
* **The right to rectification:** You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate.
* **The right to erasure:** You have the right to request that we erase your personal data (see "Delete Account" above).
